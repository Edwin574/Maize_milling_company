About our company

Maize Millers Ke is a company that aims at connecting farmers to retailers and wholesalers.We buy maize from farmers all over kenya and process the maize to produce different products.We sell Products to different companies and customers.We work at our best to be able to promote agriculture and productivity in line with the Governments agenda

Slogan

A happy farmer is a happy and healthy nation!

Mission

Our Mission is to be able to support and promote agriculture by buying products directly from farmers at good prices

Motto

We aim at providing best quality products from maize and ensuring consumer satisfaction
